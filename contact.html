Climate & Health Impacts

🌡 Heat Stress

High temperatures may increase the risk of heat exhaustion and dehydration, especially among outdoor workers and vulnerable populations.

🌧 Malaria Risk

Increased rainfall can create breeding sites for mosquitoes, potentially elevating malaria transmission risk in affected regions.

🌬 Air Quality

Wind patterns influence the dispersion of pollutants. Low wind speeds may lead to poor air quality and respiratory health concerns.

⚠ Advisory

Monitor vulnerable populations, including children and the elderly, during extreme weather conditions.
